About Nepal

Nepal, nestled between India and China, is a land of mountains, rivers, and cultural heritage. Known for Mount Everest, Lord Buddha’s birthplace, and its diverse ethnic traditions, Nepal represents unity in diversity.

From bustling Kathmandu streets to the serene Himalayas, Nepal balances ancient traditions with modern growth.
