Nepal’s Economy

Tourism 🏔️

Mountains, trekking routes, and spiritual journeys attract millions of tourists yearly.

Remittance 💵

Nepal’s economy heavily relies on remittances from millions of Nepalis working abroad.

Hydropower ⚡

With abundant rivers, Nepal is a rising energy hub for South Asia.

IT & Startups 💻

A growing wave of tech talent is pushing Nepal toward digital transformation.
