Nepalese Culture & Traditions

Festivals 🎉

Dashain, Tihar, Holi, and Buddha Jayanti unite families and communities across Nepal.

Food 🍲

Dal-Bhat, Momo, Newari dishes, and sel-roti represent Nepal’s diverse cuisines.

Heritage 🏯

Kathmandu Valley is home to UNESCO sites like Pashupatinath, Boudhanath, and Durbar Squares.
